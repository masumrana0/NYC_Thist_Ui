VALHALLA

A LITTLE ABOUT VALHALLA

This Viking themed bar has 48 tabs of craft bear and cider. They play heavy on the Viking themed with their decor, selection of Mead and imported iceland Einstok beers on tap.They have two happy hours4 -6pm and angry hour 12am - close.

With a full bar and Viking themed drink specials like the "Nordic mule" and the "Valhalla" and the "Valhalla to Hell" you are sure to have fantastic time. I would get three early and take advantage of the happy hour and to get a seat. Valhalla is not a large bar, it could get crowded and lound very fast.

The viking theme decor really gives the bar its character. When you enter the bar if you look up to you left you will see a breaded viking greeting you.I am not sure if he is angry or drunk.He does look amazing.
