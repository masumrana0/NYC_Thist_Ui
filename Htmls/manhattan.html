A LITTLE ABOUT MANHATTAN

Manhattan is the most densely populated of New York City's 5 boroughs.It's mostly made up of Manhattan Island, bounded by the Hudson,East and Harlem rivers.Among the worlds major commercial,financial and cultural centers, it's the herart of "the Big Apple".It's iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building, neon-lit Times Square and the theaters of Brodway.
